How do I build ideas? How do I make sure my opinions are reflective of an underlying reality?

Sorting through information and becoming informed on complex issues in the world is an incredibly time intensive and difficult process. It involves sorting through the obscene amount of information and perspectives that are now available on almost every topic and distilling this information into a coherent narrative. Searching, aggregating, vetting, fact-checking and synthesizing are all required to truly be informed on any topic that is worth being informed about. I built this site to help me catalogue sources that I read. This site is a personal tool to help me summarize and catalogue my sources, allowing me to see how my understanding develops and where my knowledge of topics are coming from.
